Success with INTO – a world of opportunity

Every year, around 14,000 students choose INTO. By studying with us, you can fulfil your ambition of
achieving a degree from a top UK, US or Australian university and take your first steps towards your
dream career.

With a wide choice of subject areas to progress to after completing your INTO program, you can find
perfect pathway to your chosen career. From your application to the day you graduate, we are here to
help you succeed every step of the way.

Meet Haneet from India

Haneet studied the Electrical and Computer Engineering Graduate Pathway at INTO CSU and progressed to an MS in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Colorado State University.

Today Haneet is a Functional Safety Engineer at Magna Electronics in Detroit.

Meet Ian from Malaysia

Ian began his journey at INTO Queens and progressed to a BEng in Mechanical Engineering at The University of Queens, Belfast.

After taking part in a graduate scheme at Airbus, he's now an Automation Engineer in the UK.

Meet Elizabeth from Nigeria

Elizabeth studied at INTO University of East Anglia and graduated with a Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Biomedical Sciences.

Elizabeth now works as a Donor Care Officer for a charity in Dublin whilst chasing her dream goal of working for the United Nations.

Meet Peter from Taiwan

Peter studied a Master's degree in Entrepreneurship at the University of South Florida.

Peter is the founder and CEO of Pumpkin Studio, specializing in VR content development and solutions.
