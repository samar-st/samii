About INTO - the international study specialists

Every year we help thousands of international students from around the world achieve their
dream of studying in the UK, US and China. We work with universities to create specialist
programs designed specifically for international students.

We offer exceptional support services before, during and after your studies with us, so you
can concentrate on your success story while we take care of the practicalities. You’re
guaranteed a great overseas study experience with INTO.

Ignite your career

Launch your future career today, by studying with INTO

Supporting you in your journey to success

We have expert support teams that will help you from the moment you apply, including:

Enrollment Services Counselors and Student Support Advisors are here to help you before you leave your home country. They will answer general enquiries before you apply, provide you with information about studying abroad with INTO, and help you choose the right course for you.

The Student Services team at your INTO Center is responsible for your student experience. This team ensures that you settle into your new home, make friends and have a good social life between studies. You can also enquire about accommodation and visas and receive advice about your welfare.

Your teachers understand that it takes time to adjust to living and studying in a new country. They will help you adapt to the new educational system and support you in preparing for university study. Your teachers are experts in their fields – you’ll receive all the guidance and tuition you need to succeed.

International Progression and Student Placement Officers at your INTO Center will support you in applying to university. They will work with you to find the perfect university and degree program for your needs and future ambitions, running workshops, counseling sessions and trips to schools.

With academic, emotional and practical support from INTO, you'll be able to perform at your best during your studies.

Committed to your success

The qualifications, experiences and skills you develop while studying with INTO will give you a solid foundation for the future. We've helped thousands of INTO students graduate and find rewarding and successful careers in their home countries and abroad. Are you ready to join them?

Support our charity, INTO Giving, as you study

INTO Giving is a charity that helps thousands of the world’s most disadvantaged children. We’ve raised more than $1 million to give schoolchildren hope and the chance to succeed through education.

Get involved with the INTO Giving student ambassador program while studying at INTO to enhance your CV/resume and boost your employability. Take part in INTO Giving activities at your INTO Center to meet students from across the world and raise money for great causes.
